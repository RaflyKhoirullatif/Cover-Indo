About

Cover Indo is a seller who provides various kinds of needs to protect motorbikes, such as motorbike covers and seat covers. There are many color and motif variants sold. For motorbike covers, there are also motorbike covers that use ears to protect the motorbike mirrors. The seat covers also have a variety of motifs, some are plain, have AMV on them, have motorbike logos on them, and some have motifs such as pistons, wolves, etc.
